# Daily Workflows

This guide covers recommended daily workflows to get the most out of Noteece.

---

## Morning Routine

### 1. Quick Review (5 minutes)

1. Open the **Dashboard**
2. Check the **Due Today** widget for urgent tasks
3. Review yesterday's incomplete items
4. Glance at your **Goals** progress

### 2. Plan Your Day (10 minutes)

1. Open **Tasks** or your daily note
2. Identify your top 3 priorities
3. Time-block if using calendar integration
4. Set a **Focus Timer** for your first task

---

## Throughout the Day

### Capture Everything

- Use **Quick Capture** (`Cmd/Ctrl + Shift + N`) for fleeting thoughts
- Tag notes for easy retrieval later
- Link related notes with `[[WikiLinks]]`

### Stay Focused

1. Use the **Focus Timer** widget (Pomodoro technique)
2. Enable **Focus Mode** to hide distractions
3. Log your **Mood** during breaks

### Track Progress

- Check off completed tasks
- Update project milestones
- Log time entries if using Time Tracking

---

## Evening Review

### 1. Daily Reflection (10 minutes)

1. Review completed tasks
2. Move incomplete items to tomorrow
3. Log your daily mood and energy level
4. Note any insights or learnings

### 2. Prepare for Tomorrow

1. Review tomorrow's calendar
2. Identify top priorities
3. Capture any lingering thoughts

---

## Weekly Review

### Every Sunday (30 minutes)

1. **Review the Week:**
   - Open **Weekly Progress** widget
   - Review completed tasks and projects
   - Check habit streaks

2. **Process Inbox:**
   - Review Quick Capture notes
   - File or delete as appropriate
   - Create tasks from action items

3. **Plan Ahead:**
   - Review upcoming deadlines
   - Set goals for the week
   - Block focus time on calendar

4. **Maintain:**
   - Archive completed projects
   - Review and update goals
   - Sync across devices

---

## Pro Tips

### Use Templates

Create note templates for:
- Daily journals
- Meeting notes
- Project kickoffs
- Weekly reviews

### Leverage AI

- Ask the AI Assistant to summarize long notes
- Generate task lists from meeting notes
- Get insights from your data

### Build Habits

- Track habits consistently
- Review streaks weekly
- Celebrate milestones
